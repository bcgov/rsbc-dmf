Find below the various types of decision outcomes
  • Fit to Drive – Driver is fit to drive
  • Unfit to Drive – Driver is not fit to drive
  • FDE Referral – Driver has been referred for a Functional Driving Evaluation (FDE)
  • Non-comply – Driver has not complied to requirements
  • Deceased – Associated Driver is deceased
  • No Action taken – Case has been reviewed and no action is required
  • Voluntary Surrender - All Privileges indicates the driver has voluntarily chosen to surrender his/her licence class, rather than complete the DMER or other fitness requirement. If they want to re-licence, he/she must first complete the outstanding requirement.
