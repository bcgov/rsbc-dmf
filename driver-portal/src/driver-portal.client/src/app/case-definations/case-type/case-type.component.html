Find below the various case types.
  • Driver's Medical Examination Report – Cases opened because a Driver's Medical Examination Report was issued.
  • Reported Medical Condition - Cases opened due to receiving a high-priority medical report.
  • Police Report – Cases opened due to receiving a police report related to medical fitness to drive.
  • Unsolicited Report - Cases opened due to receiving a report from a member of the public raising road safety concerns.
  • Other - Cases opened due to receiving other documents not noted above
  • RSBC - Cases manually created/opened by RoadSafetyBC staff
  • Legacy - Legacy cases migrated from the old legacy system
