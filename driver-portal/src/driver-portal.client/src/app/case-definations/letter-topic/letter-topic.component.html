Find below descriptions of the various letter topics.
  • DMER-Aviation Certificate – A letter informing the driver that the medical certificate they submitted is not acceptable and a completed DMER is required.
  • Foreign DMER – A letter informing the driver that the DMER they submitted is not acceptable and another one is required.
  • DMER Completed by Improper Professional – A letter informing the driver that the DMER they submitted is not acceptable and another one is required.
  • DMER-Missing Physician Info - A letter informing the driver that the DMER they submitted is missing Physician info and require a signed DMER.
  • DMER- New Address-Cancelled DL - A letter informing the driver that their driver’s licence is currently cancelled for non-compliance of the medical requirement associated with holding a BC Driver’s License and a DMER is required.
  • DMER-OMI Over 12 Months- Non Comply - A letter informing the driver that due to a system error, their DL should have been cancelled for non-compliance of their DMER and a new DMER has been issued.
  • DMER-Reinstate DL - A letter informing the driver their DMER was not received within the required timeframe and their driver’s licence was cancelled. Driver may attend a Driver Licensing Office to reinstate their DL.
  • DMER-system Error-DMER Not Scheduled - A letter informing the driver that due to a system error, their DMER was not issued and they will receive their normally scheduled DMER within 12 months.
  • Request for Updated DMER - A letter informing the driver that the DMER they submitted is not acceptable and another one is required.
  • DMER-Transport Canada Medical Report - A letter informing the driver their Transportation Canada Medical Examination will be accepted if both pages of the report or certificate are submitted
  • Adding R21 - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class, based on conditions.
  • Fit Class 5/7 - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class.
  • Commercial Fit - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class.
  • Commercial Fit Diabetes - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class, based on conditions.
  • Fit Diabetes with R21 - A letter approving the licensing of the driver to be fit for a certain class, based on certain conditions.
  • Fit EVF Unlicensed- A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class.
  • Fit Removing R21 - a letter approving the licensing of the driver to be fit for a certain class without restriction, based on conditions
  • Fit Requiring Conditions Class 5/7 - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class, based on conditions.
  • Fit Requiring Conditions Class 5/7 R21 - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class, based on conditions.
  • Commercial Fit Requiring Conditions - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class, based on conditions.
  • Commercial Fit Requiring Conditions R21 - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class, based on conditions.
  • Fit Seizure Requiring Conditions Class 5/7 Active DL - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class, based on conditions.
  • Fit Seizure Requiring Conditions Class 5/7 NO Active DL - A letter approving the Licensing of the Driver to be fit for a certain class, based on conditions.
