Find below the various types of case statuses.
  • Opened - This case has been opened by requesting a document (as opposed to receiving a submission), and has not yet had any documents submitted to it.
  • Open Pending Submission – – DMER has been started by a medical practitioner, but it has not been submitted to RoadSafetyBC.
  • Under Review – The case has at least one document submitted to it, and has not yet rendered a decision.
  • Decision Rendered – A decision has been rendered, and a (usually) 30 day timer is underway for follow-ups, challenges, or additional documents that might change things.
  • Resolved – The case is now closed. If new information is received, a new case will be opened rather than adding to this case.
