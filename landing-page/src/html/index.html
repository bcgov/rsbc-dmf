Welcome to RoadSafetyBC's Driver Medical Fitness Portal RoadSafetyBC is part of the Ministry of Public Safety and Solicitor General and is responsible for road safety in the province. We govern drivers, implement road safety policies, and work with our partners to help reach our goal of zero traffic fatalities.
Learn More

To help fulfill our responsibility for assessing and determining driver medical fitness, RoadSafetyBC may request an individual to complete a Driver’s Medical Examination Report (DMER), which is the principal assessment tool used to help determine if a driver’s medical condition affects their ability to drive safely.
